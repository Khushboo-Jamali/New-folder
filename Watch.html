A smartwatch is a wearable computing device that closely resembles a wristwatch or other time-keeping device. In addition to telling time, many smartwatches are Bluetooth-capable. The watch becomes a
wireless Bluetooth adaptor capable of extending the capabilities of the wearer's smartphone to the watch. If the smartwatch has a built-in GPS and/or heart rate sensor, the watch tracks your daily activities. This includes counting the number of steps, stairs, calories burned, and sleep pattern. Thanks to the GPS, a watch also measures your route while you exercise.
